About Lytle Electric

Founded in Colorado Springs by Megan and John Lytle, Lytle Electric is rooted in Southern Colorado service with decades of hands-on electrical contracting experience.

Professional Excellence

Rigorous compliance and safety standards underscored by meticulous documentation practices. Every license, inspection, and affiliation is verified and up to date.

Community Commitment

Active engagement with SCWCC Foundation Board and Young Professionals Committee, supporting scholarships and nonprofit work throughout Colorado.

Transparent Operations

Commitment to transparent legacy archiving with publicly accessible repository of projects, press recognition, and community partnerships.

Core Values

Quality craftsmanship, ethical integrity, and enduring community trust. Evolution from active field operations to compliance-driven consulting and legacy management.
